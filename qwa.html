QUEST
WEB APP
REDESIGN.

Redesigning a 21st century interactive learning platform for marginalized children and youth.

Quest Alliance is a social venture that works towards creating powerful teaching experiences for marginalized children and youth to realize their full potential.

As an UI/UX Design intern at HumanX, I got an opportunity to work on the QUEST App, an interactive learning platform to impart 21st century skills to learners.

Type
Internship Project

Timeframe
May-Aug ’20

Role
UI/UX Designer

Platform
Mobile & Web

Overview

We create learning experiences that inspire and empower educators and learners alike.

  • Quality Education & Skills Training Alliance is a social venture that works towards creating powerful learning experiences. QUEST App is an interactive learning platform to impart 21st century skills to learners making them more confident, adaptable & hence more productive.
  • QUEST Learning centres adopt a “Blended learning model”, where students are physically present in the centre, & are taught using the QUEST App. Therefor a very basic & merely functional app was also enough for the day to day teaching at the centres.
  • But due to the ongoing pandemic, the physical centres couldn't operate. Therefore, the students had to totally rely on the QUEST App. That's why the need for a redesign arose.

Target Audience

  • A majority of such users have low tech exposure & are generally not comfortable with complex applications.
  • About 80% of the current batch of users didn’t have access to a laptop or tablet at home. But almost all of them had a smartphone in their families.
  • During the onsite workshops conducted by the team (which I wasn't a part of), it was found that most of the users were highly accustomed to the app interfaces of Tiktok & Facebook, because they had been using these apps for a long time.

My Role

  • I became a part of this project in May, 2020. By this time, the team had already conducted onsite workshops and 1-to-1 interviews with the learners, trainers and other key stakeholders. I tried my best to understand all the insights they had gathered.
  • I was a part of the discussions on user flows and the wireframes. Due to various constraints from the stakeholders, we couldn't alter the flows to a great extent, but the wireframes were finalized after countless iterations.
  • I had a major role in the interface design phase, where I finalised the UI style and designed the screens for a majority of the flows.

The Problems

  • The blended mode of teaching kept the learners motivated and encouraged to keep going and complete their subjects. The motivation levels might get decreased due to the completely online education.
  • It is difficult for teachers to keep track of the progress of the students in their batch, and share information with them.
  • Due to online learning, it would be difficult for students to clear their doubts.
  • Loss of student-student interaction was also a factor that could lead to students losing interest.
  • Poor internet connectivity, students not having access to laptops, general chaos during the pandemic etc, were also problems that needed to be addressed.

Some Key Decisions

A number of solutions for the mentioned problems were proposed. The target audience was a major factor in filtering out viable solutions.


  • Around 80% of the students did not own their personal computers/laptops. Therefore a mobile app also became essential (as most of the students did own smartphones).
  • To compensate for the loss of student-teacher interaction, a Trainer-side app also had to be designed. This would allow for better progress tracking by trainers and smoother management of the students under them.

The following are the broad decisions that we landed on. These points were kept in mind while designing the various flows as well as the interface of the app —

  1. Simplified journey with progress tracking -

    The user flows had to be kept very simple and straightforward, as the target audience had low tech exposure and computer literacy. Breadcrumb elements were placed so that the user doesn't get lost in the app. Various elements useful in tracking personal progress were added at each step, so as to keep the learners encouraged and motivated.
